SPORTS ANIME AND FASHION

Summary:

      A fascinating topic that addresses the dynamic
interaction between these two seemingly unconnected
fields is fashionin sports and anime. Sports and anime
both have a big influence on world culture and trends,
and their fusion has given rise to distinctive signs of
fashion that not only affecthow participants and
viewers dress but also public relations(PR) operations
. This literature review discusses how sports fashion
and anime overlap , examines how these two
genreshave influenced one another , and considers
how public relations (PR) might help to promote this
fusion.
